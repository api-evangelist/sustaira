For many IT teams, sustainability data started as a peripheral concern handled in spreadsheets or a standalone CSR tool managed outside core systems. That has changed. With regulations such as CSRD in Europe and increasing expectations around financial-grade climate disclosures, sustainability data is now part of enterprise reporting and risk infrastructure.
