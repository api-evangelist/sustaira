For decades, the manufacturing sector measured competitiveness through a predictable formula: unit cost, lead time, and quality. In the 1980s and 1990s, the adoption of Lean Manufacturing reshaped this formula, turning operational efficiency and waste reduction into critical requirements for winning business. Today, a similar shift is taking place across the global industrial sector.
