New solution enables organizations to centralize, standardize, and automate waste data management across sites, streams, and disposal methods. Sustaira, the modular AI-powered sustainability platform, today announced the launch of its new Waste Management Solution a solution designed to help organizations manage and analyze waste data with greater accuracy, efficiency, and transparency. As sustainability regulations evolve and reporting requirements become more stringent, organizations face increasing pressure to improve the way they track and report waste.
