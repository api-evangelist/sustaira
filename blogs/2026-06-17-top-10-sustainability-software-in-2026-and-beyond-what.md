A buyer's guide evaluating ten leading sustainability platforms by enterprise buyer priorities, positioning Sustaira as a modular, AI-powered, industry-tailored leader. Competitors reviewed include Watershed (carbon-centric), Workiva (disclosure), Salesforce Net Zero Cloud (CRM-integrated), Sweep (value-chain transparency), SAP Sustainability Control Tower (ERP-native), IBM Envizi (data analytics), Sphera (EHS compliance), Osapiens (supply chain regulations), and Persefoni (financial reporting). It argues platform selection is now a corporate-level decision requiring IT alignment and should be assessed across functional, technical, and organizational dimensions.
