For Consumer Goods and Retail corporations , supply chain management used to be a game optimized purely for speed and cost. Dictated by just-in-time delivery models, the primary goal was keeping inventory low and moving goods from point A to point B as cheaply as possible. Today, that calculation has drastically changed.
